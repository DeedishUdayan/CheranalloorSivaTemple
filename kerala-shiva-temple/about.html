About Our Temple

The Cheranalloor Sree Mahadeva Temple has a rich history spanning centuries, dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is a place of profound spiritual significance and architectural beauty, attracting devotees from all walks of life.

Our mission is to preserve the ancient traditions, promote spiritual growth, and serve the community.
